Professional Role

From January 2024 to March 2025, I worked at Williams Tharp Architecture as a Project Coordinator. My contributions ranged from rendering and modeling to participating in early-stage programming, design documentation, and internal reviews. I was exposed to collaborative workflows and real-world decision-making that shaped how we addressed client goals within code and budget constraints.
